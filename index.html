Narrative

Visualizations will include interesting views on cinematography datasets. The goal is to show the movies data from different perspectives.
Visualizations will be divided on two topics. One visualization is focused on locations of movies filmed in San Francisco. Second is focused on movie ratings analysis.
In general, when you think of locations where the movies masteprieces were filmed, Los Angeles and New York come to mind first.
